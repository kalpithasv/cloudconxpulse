Event description

GameOn Bidz

Step into the electrifying world of cricket fervor with our college cultural event, 'GameOn Bidz-Where every bid spins a cricket tale.' Witness the amalgamation of sports enthusiasm and cultural vibrancy as students come together to experience the thrill of a simulated IPL auction. It's not just about bids and players; it's a celebration of teamwork, strategy, and the sheer joy of the game.

Rules

RULES

1. round one is quiz (based on ipl) to filter all the teams and top 10 teams to go into auction.
2. then you get a team at random choice and one of that teams players can be retained as a captain.
3. before auction date player sets will be released with base price to study the auction tactics as only those players will be used in auction.
4. the auction starts from the given set of players already given with different sets.
5. the winners will be selected based on the team balance,form of the players and the common stats as the playing 11 will be the final team.
6. must have 5 bowling option, a wicketkeeper and must have at least one allrounder (only 4 foreign players).
