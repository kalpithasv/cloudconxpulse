Event description

LEAGUE of BRAINS

Welcome to the League of Brains, where your game development knowledge is put to the test! This quiz will challenge your understanding of various aspects of game development, from programming languages to design principles.

Sharpen your virtual pencils and get ready to showcase your expertise in the dynamic world of creating games. Compete with fellow students and prove that you have the brains to dominate the League of Game development.

Level 1: Preliminary round
The question is asked to all
First Team to lift their board get to answer

Level 2: rapid fire round
Questions are asked to teams , the team that answers gets a point or is passed to other team

Level 3: elimination round
After every round , each team with least points is dismissed until 2 teams are left

Level 4: Classical Stand-off
2 Teams remain who find themselves in a stand off . question is asked , the first team to hit the bell and answer the question gets the point, first team to reach 5 points wins.

Rules

RULES

1. Minimum of 3 members per team.
2. Interaction between any 2 teams will disqualify both the teams
3. If any team falls short of its team members, the team is disqualified
4. The question will be repeated only twice
5. A team can stay after disqualification, but cannot contact any remaining team
6. Using of phones during the quiz will be considered cheating
